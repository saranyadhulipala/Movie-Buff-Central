Iron Man

Real Name: Tony Stark

Abilities: Genius-level intellect, skilled engineer and inventor, powered armor suit with advanced technology.

Notable Films: Iron Man, The Avengers, Avengers: Endgame.

Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist, becomes the armored superhero Iron Man after building a high-tech suit to escape captivity. He uses his intellect and resources to protect the world from various threats.

Thor

Real Name: Thor Odinson

Abilities: God-like strength, control over thunder and lightning, skilled warrior, wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir.

Notable Films: Thor, Thor: Ragnarok, Avengers: Infinity War.

The God of Thunder, Thor, is a powerful warrior from Asgard who wields Mjolnir, his enchanted hammer. He defends both Asgard and Earth from numerous threats, often alongside his fellow Avengers.

Captain America

Real Name: Steve Rogers

Abilities: Enhanced strength, agility, and durability from the Super Soldier Serum, expert tactician and hand-to-hand combatant, iconic shield.

Notable Films: Captain America: The First Avenger, The Avengers, Captain America: Civil War.

Steve Rogers, a scrawny young man, becomes the super-soldier Captain America after being injected with the Super Soldier Serum during World War II. He fights for justice and freedom, leading the Avengers against formidable foes.

Black Widow

Real Name: Natasha Romanoff

Abilities: Expert spy and assassin, master martial artist, skilled marksman.

Notable Films: Iron Man 2, The Avengers, Black Widow.

Natasha Romanoff, known as Black Widow, is a former Russian spy turned Avenger. She uses her combat skills and espionage expertise to fight for justice alongside her fellow heroes.
